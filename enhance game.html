ABOUT THE GAMES

TIC TAC TOE

  • Classic 3×3 strategy game
  • Play against a friend or computer AI
  • Three AI difficulty levels: Easy, Medium, Hard
  • Score tracking across multiple rounds
  • Win detection for rows, columns, and diagonals

WORD GUESS

  • Hangman-style word guessing game
  • All words are computer/tech related terms
  • 6 attempts per word — each wrong guess draws a body part
  • Score = remaining attempts × 10 per win
  • Click keyboard or press letter keys to guess

CONTROLS

  • CLICK — Primary interaction in all games
  • LETTER KEYS — Guess letters in Word Guess
  • R — Restart round in Tic Tac Toe
  • ESC — Return to menu from any game
  • NUMBER KEYS [1–5] — Navigate main menu
